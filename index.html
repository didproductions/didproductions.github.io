D.I.D. ?

or ‘D. Idea’ Productions – offers audio-visual media with a funky-fresh touch. The company was founded by D. Kerremans, a creative filmmaker and photographer with a sharp eye for style and story-telling. On an epic trip to the World Cup in South-Africa some of his family and he found a mysterious solution for the (back then always failing) Belgian national football-team, and made a documentary about it. Enjoy this short preview of The Red Devils’ Secret, and discover how thanks to magical plants, tribal rituals and ostrich rodeos D.I.D.’s logo was born.
